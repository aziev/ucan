VEIW DETAILS

The Level 1 Functional Skills qualification in English is an ideal qualification for learners who wish to develop their English skills for use across a broad range of contexts. The qualification will encourage learners to demonstrate their ‘functional English’ skills and are essentially concerned with developing and recognising the ability of learners to apply and transfer skills in ways that are appropriate to their situation. The aims of the qualification are to enable learners to promote the development of English skills for home, leisure and work

The qualification consists of 3 components that are individually assessed using a mixture of internal and external assessments. The course is flexible in design enabling the learner to attempt the formal assessments on an on-demand basis when they feel ready. We provide an 8 session training course, delivered in the workplace by a method of blended learning.
